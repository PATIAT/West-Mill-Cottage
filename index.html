Stay at West Mill Cottage

West Mill is a charming cottage set in a picturesque rural location. It is one of two cottages located on the banks of a large meander in the River Whiteadder just outside the village of Paxton. Set at the end of a no-through road three miles west of Berwick-upon-Tweed, England’s northernmost town, and just east of the river from Paxton, a small Saxon village set in Scotland, West Mill Cottage is a true Borders home.

The river, which flows just outside the cottage windows, is bubbling with wildlife - watch the local otter hunt for trout and salmon or observe the graceful white swans who nest nearby. Chances are you’ll also spot deer grazing outside or catch sight of a heron flashing along the riverbank. The ever-changing scenery doesn’t even stop when night falls. West Mill Cottage lies below some of the country’s most pristine dark skies, which make for an unforgettable experience.
