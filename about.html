How To Get Here

By Car

West Mill is best accessed by car and is located at the end of a no through countryside road. The cottage is approximately 5 miles from Berwick Upon Tweed and is just off the A1.

By Train

The nearest train station is located in Berwick Upon Tweed. The station is a frequent stop for trains on the east coast main line and is a short 15 minute taxi journey away.

By Bus

The nearest bus service stop is Berwick Upon Tweed which is located just off the A1 so is a frequent stop on many of the main east coast routes between Newcastle and Edinburgh.
